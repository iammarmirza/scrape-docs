Account Management
==================

You can add and manage multiple accounts from different VCS providers in the DeepSource dashboard. Click on the account switcher on the top left.

![](https://files.readme.io/a389bb9-Click-Account-switcher.png)

Individual Accounts

[](#individual-accounts)
===============================================

An individual or personal account is tied to a personal GitHub, Gitlab, Bitbucket, or Azure DevOps Services account.

Organization or Team Accounts

[](#organization-or-team-accounts)
===================================================================

An Organization or Team account is tied to a GitHub org, GitLab group, or Bitbucket workspace.

Adding a new account

[](#adding-a-new-account)
-------------------------------------------------

Add one or more team accounts and/or personal accounts from the supported VCS providers to the DeepSource dashboard. Click on 'Add an account' in the account switcher.

![](https://files.readme.io/3c3b0c2-Adding_an_account.png)

Signing out of an account

[](#signing-out-of-an-account)
-----------------------------------------------------------

Click on your username at the bottom-left of the dashboard and click on ‘Sign out’.

![](https://files.readme.io/a49ed38-Signing_out_of_an_account.png)
