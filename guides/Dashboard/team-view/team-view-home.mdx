Recently active repositories

[](#recently-active-repositories)
-----------------------------------------------------------------

Repositories that were recently activated will be listed here along with the Analyzers that were enabled for analysis. The + button on the top-right corner can be used to activate new repositories.

![](https://files.readme.io/1379810-recently_active_repos.png)

Pinned reports

[](#pinned-reports)
-------------------------------------

In this section, you may pin any 4 of the 6 DeepSource Reports that are the most meaningful and actionable to you. These reports reflect organization-wide data. You can change the reports and/or customize the period of the reported data by clicking on the ‘...’ icon.

![](https://files.readme.io/459fccd-pinned-reports.png)
