Editor
======

GitBook’s editor supports different writing formats, interactive blocks, helpful ways to organize your content and more.

Last updated 5 months ago

GitBook offers a range of block types for you to add to your content inline — from simple text and tables, to code blocks and more. These elements will make your pages more useful to readers, and offer extra information and context.

And when it comes to organizing your content, you have multiple options depending on your needs. Find out more below.

**Permissions** Only users with can edit pages.
