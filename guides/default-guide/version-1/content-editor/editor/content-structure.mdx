Content structure
=================

Learn how to use pages, page groups, spaces and collections

The structure of your content in GitBook is organized through pages, spaces and collections. Pages live inside of spaces, and collections are groups of spaces.

**Permissions**

Anyone with and higher can create pages. People with and higher can create new spaces and collections.
